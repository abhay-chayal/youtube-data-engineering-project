S3 Data Lake Structure

Bucket Name: youtube-data-engineering-bucket

youtube-data-engineering-bucket/
│
├── raw/
│   └── statistics/
│       ├── region=us/
│       ├── region=in/
│       ├── region=gb/
│
├── cleansed/
│   └── statistics_parquet/
│       ├── region=us/
│       ├── region=in/
│       ├── region=gb/
│
└── curated/
    └── analytics_ready/
        ├── region=us/
        ├── region=in/
        ├── region=gb/

Explanation:
- raw/ → Original JSON files uploaded
- cleansed/ → Converted Parquet files
- curated/ → Partitioned & analytics-ready data
- region partitioning improves Athena performance

Data Source:
	Kaggle youtube Dataset
